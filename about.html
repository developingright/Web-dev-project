About Us

Our Company ~ Galaxy Burger started in 2090 , when we used to provide our services on earth with our branches in over 125 countries. Our main focus is on our fast food that we make.

From the start, we've been committed to doing the right thing. And every day, all around the globe, we put people, processes and practices into place to make quality food, more responsible sourcing choices, a stronger community and a better planet.

We’re passionate about our food and take great care that what we serve every day is safe, great quality, offers choice and is produced in a responsible way.
